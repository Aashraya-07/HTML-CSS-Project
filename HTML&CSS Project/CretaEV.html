Hyundai has launched the Creta Electric for the Nepali market

Hyundai Creta Electric Overview

The Hyundai Creta Electric represents a bold step into the electric vehicle market for Hyundai in Nepal. Building on the popularity of its ICE counterpart, the Creta Electric combines modern design with impressive electric performance. It features a choice of two battery options: a 42 kWh variant with a 99 kW motor and a longer-range 51.4 kWh option with a 126 kW motor. With a sleek exterior, a futuristic interior, and advanced features like Level 2 ADAS, the Creta Electric is designed to offer an efficient, eco-friendly driving experience in the growing EV market of Nepal.

Design

The Hyundai Creta Electric combines the bold design of its ICE counterpart with modern electric vehicle aesthetics. The front features vertically stacked Dual-Barrel LED headlights, a pixelated grille, and active air flaps for improved aerodynamics. The rear boasts a distinctive LED light bar and a sloping roofline that adds profile is dynamic, sitting on sleek 17-inch Aero alloy wheels. At the rear, the taillights are similar to the standard Creta, but with unique touches like a black accent, pixel-style bumper detailing, and a faux silver skid plate. Inside, the dual-tone cabin includes a 10.25-inch digital instrument cluster and infotainment screen, with a drive selector knob replacing the traditional gear stick. The Creta Electric is available in multiple color options, including both metallic and matte finishes.
