Deepal has launched the S07 for the Nepali market

Deepal S07 Overview

The Deepal S07 is a flagship electric SUV that highlights Deepal's commitment to blending luxury, performance, and cutting-edge technology. Launched as part of the brand's expanding lineup in 2023, the S07 quickly gained attention for its sleek design, spacious interior, and advanced features such as gesture control and an augmented reality heads-up display. It reflects Deepal's human-centered approach to innovation, offering a premium driving experience while supporting sustainability. The S07 has also made a strong impact in international markets, particularly in Nepal, where it earned the title of "SUV of the Year 2024" and attracted over 800 customers within its first yearâ€”signaling strong consumer interest in high-end EVs tailored to modern lifestyles.

Design

Deepal S07 sports a wide-body design, provoking pure simplicity and ultimate dynamic. It features the Moon String Front Face with well-defined ribs and completely frameless doors with flush door handles. The front section has a complex LED system (though Nepal specific variant misses out on Intelligent Interactive LED DRLs), while the rear comes with a Starflame Through-Type Taillight with an Illuminated Logo.
