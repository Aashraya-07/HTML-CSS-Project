BYD has launched the Atto 3 for the Nepali market

BYD Atto 3 Overview

The BYD Atto 3 is a popular electric SUV in Nepal, offering a perfect blend of style, performance, and sustainability. Known for its futuristic design and advanced features, the Atto 3 comes with a 60.48 kWh battery that provides a range of up to 420 km on a single charge. The vehicle boasts a sleek, modern exterior with sharp lines and a spacious, tech-driven interior, including a 12.8-inch rotating infotainment screen. With a focus on safety, it comes equipped with Level 2 ADAS, multiple airbags, and other safety features. The Atto 3 has gained traction in Nepal's EV market, offering a well-rounded, eco-friendly driving experience at a competitive price point.

Design

The BYD Atto 3 features a futuristic design with sharp, angular lines that give it a distinctive road presence. The front is dominated by a sleek LED light bar that connects the dragon-inspired headlights, while the rear features a full-width LED light strip. The aerodynamic design includes flush door handles and a smooth underbody to maximize efficiency. Inside, the Atto 3 offers a unique "gym-inspired" interior with bold colors, flexible materials, and a rotating 12.8-inch touchscreen that serves as the command center for the vehicle's advanced tech features.
